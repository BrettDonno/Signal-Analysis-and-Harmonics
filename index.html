Introduction to harmonics - Fourier Series

Any perodic signal can be represented by a series of sinusodial signals consisting of, 1) a fundamental component, the frequency with the highest amplitied called the fundamental frequency and 2) various harmonic components with magnitude, frequency and phase.
Note the fundamental frequency is the desired frequency to be generated (e.g. generators spinning at 50 Hz) while the harmonics are unintended components.
